Ny Strip Steak, Mac n Cheese

  • Time: 1 hour.
Meal Ingredients Utensils
Ny Strip Steak n Mac n Cheese
  • Elbow Noodles
  • Carnation milk, flour, 1 egg, Cheddar cheese, gouda, Extra Sharp Cheddar and Marble Cheese.
  • New York strip steak
  • Minced Garlic, Pepper, Ground Garlic and Onion, Laury's seasoning salt, All Seasoning.
-Oven
-Pot
-Gratter
Step #1 Preheat oven to 350. Take a bowl and then add a mixture of cold water and apple cider vinegar. Place your steaks in that mixture for three minutes and then rinse it off. Bowl
Step #2 Once the steak is cleaned, season both sides and massage the seasoning in to your liking and place it in a pan with 2 table spoons of butter,put it in the oven for 45 minutes Pan
Step #3 Grab a pot and put water on to boil mixed with salt and oil. While waiting for that to boil, shred all of the cheese. Once the water comes to a boil, add 2 boxes of elbow noodles Gratter, Pot , Bowl
